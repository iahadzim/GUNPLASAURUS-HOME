T H E
S I D E C U T T E R

The most essential tool for GUNPLA Building. The side cutter is your best friend to seperate each GUNPLA parts from the plastic runner included in the box. The sharpness of the side cutter will make clean cut that will leave necxt to no mark on your GUNPLA.


T H E
P E N - K N I F E

To make your build more professional and clean looking, the pen-knife will do the job for you. One of the techniques in GUNPLA Building for cutting out parts from the runner, is to leave out a bit of plastic after cutting it out with the side cutter. Then, you will use the pen-knife to slice off the remaining bit. This will leave a cleaner surface on your GUNPLA.



T H E
T W E E Z E R

The tweezer will be handy when applying stickers to your GUNPLA Model Kit. Some GUNPLA kits lack in color separation but with BANDAI's printing technology, stickers were included to accommodate such occasion. Apllying stickers can be a hassle in hand but with a tweezer, the precision is highly increased.



T H E
G U N P L A M A R K E R

The GUNPLA Model Kit you have assembled will be in need of extra details. The Gundam Marker was made just for that. With its fine tip, you can perform panel-lining on your GUNPLA with incredible precision and details. A GUNPLA Model Kit that has been panel-lined stands out much more than those who has not.
